The space race began when our former alien overlords (The Silence) required us to begin building them space suits as we possessed minerals they did not but as we all know they were in the end thwarted due to the efforts of the eleventh doctor and his travelling companions: Amy Pond, Rory Williams and River Song.

Despite being one of the elevenths shortest arcs it is rememberd very fondly, infact some people consider the 'Silence' arc to be his best. From the characters to the acting to the dialogue it is easy to see why would consider it be so, myself among them
It is also the first time in doctor who in which we see the doctor directly affect his own future through his future interacting with his past(say that ten times fast), which does raise some question about the integrity of the story considering this means the doctor could have done in the past but either couldn't figure out how or even worse just decided not to.

Edvard Munch's 1893 painting 'The Scream' served as the inspiration for 'The Silence' as can be seen by the elongated body and face with large eyes alien shaped head.
